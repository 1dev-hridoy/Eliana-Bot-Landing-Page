About ElianaBot

ElianaBot is a free, open-source project designed to enhance the Discord experience for communities around the globe. Our mission is to provide a versatile and customizable bot that meets the diverse needs of server owners and their members.

With a rich array of features including moderation, entertainment, and utility commands, DiscordBot aims to make your server more engaging and manageable. Users can easily play music, conduct polls, or even get trivia questions, all tailored to enhance interactivity within the community.
